Indocyber is Announced a Partnership with KewMann

In November 2020, Malaysia Digital Economy Corporation (MDEC) has announced partnership between Indocyber with KewMann, an Artificial Intelligence and big data company that leverage on behavioral science to optimize outcome to help more organizations. This partnership goal is to help more organizations successfully embark into Artificial Intelligence (AI) & big data analytics journey to achieve their desired goals and objectives.

OIO appointed by Indocyber Global Teknologi

Indocyber Global Teknologi (“Indocyber”) announce a new project with Singapore Exchange-listed company OIO Holdings Limited (“OIO”) to digitize academic certificates in Indonesia on the blockchain.

FINHACK 2019

PT Bank Central Asia,Tbk menyelenggarakan sebuah rangkaian acara seputar Teknologi Informasi (TI) yang diadakan setiap tahun yang dinamakan Finhacks, final-nya diadakan pada tanggal 23 November 2019 yang bertempat di The Hall, Senayan City, lantai 8, Jakarta Selatan. Disini semua para praktisi TI berkumpul, berkolaborasi dan berinovasi dalam menciptakan solusi yang inovatif dalam bidang finansial. Di tahun ini tema yang diangkat adalah inovasi yang berbasis teknologi Blockchain.
